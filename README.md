⚠️ Important Notice About Alcohol
There is no risk-free level of alcohol consumption. Alcohol is a cellular toxin that can damage organs even in small amounts and increases the risk of over 200 diseases—including cancer and cardiovascular diseases. It also has a high potential for dependence, which can develop gradually.

Here you can find help in Germany:
By calling 01806 31 30 31 (charges apply: 20 cents per call from a landline, max. 60 cents per call from mobile), you can receive anonymous advice 24/7.
At www.kenn-dein-limit.de
 you can find self-assessment tests, tips for reducing consumption, and directories of counseling centers near you.
